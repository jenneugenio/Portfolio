Background

I won't list out my full resume but will share my background because it's not technical and non-traditional when you think of coders! As mentioned, I graduated from WSU with a Hospitality Business Management Major. During college and after graduation, I held various hospitality roles listed below:

  • Restaurant Manager
  • Sales Coordinator
  • Catering Coordinator
  • Sales Manager
  • Conference Services Manager

I truly love hospitality because of the interaction you get with a diverse group of people but I was finding the long hours and stress to be draining. I still wanted to be in my field but for a different industry so when I found my current job, it was a perfect mix of both. I now work for a cybersecurity company planning Community Events around the world and engaging with security researchers. At this job, I was finding that I had a disconnect on the technical side so I decided to sign up for a workshop so I had a foundation and basic understanding technically. This is what led me to looking at coding bootcamps but what is keeping me is my curiousity. I never thought I'd enjoy coding and want to do more so when I found myself eager to learn, I enrolled in Epicodus.
